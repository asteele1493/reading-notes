These are questions I've asked my dog today.

Imagine-- waking up to a sunlit room. Birds are chirping. You think to yourself, gasp I think I deserve a latte. You do your hair, gather your keys, and a sad pup looks up at you wondering if she'll go too.
These prompts are a reenactment of my coffee run this morning. Please note, there are literally questions I asked my dog.
Question 1: Do you want to go on a car ride?
Question 2: Was that a hiccup?
Question 3: Wait... did you just throw up in the car?
Question 4: Do you still deserve a treat?
